Presentation Syncing for Google Slides

Instructions

  1. Drag the bookmarklet above into your bookmarks bar (⌘/Ctrl-Shift-B)
  2. Open a Google Slides presentation
  3. Click the [SlideSync ▷] bookmarklet
  4. Click Host if you are the presenter or Watch to sync with the presenter.
